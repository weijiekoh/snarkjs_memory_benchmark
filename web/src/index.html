snarkjs proof generation benchmarks

This tool measures the time it takes to generate a Groth16 proof for a given circuit size. Note that it does not measure the time it takes to download the WASM and zkey files. A slight warmup time (0.1s) tends to be incurred upon the first run. The circuit takes two field elements as an input, and outputs their Fibonacci sequence over the prime field. The witness generation timings may differ if the circuit changes.

On a mobile device, this page will likely crash at the circuit size of 131072, and on a laptop, 2097152 (depending on the device).

Glossary:

  • # constraints: The number of circuit constraints.
  • WASM size: The size of the WASM witness generation binary.
  • zkey size: The size of the zkey file, mostly comprised of the proving key.
  • Witness computation: The time taken to generate the witness using the WASM binary.
  • Proof generation: The time taken to generate the proof.
  • ABC: The time taken to run buildABC1() (this has to do with the QAP).
  • MSM A: The time taken to perform the G1 MSM to generate point A.
  • MSM B1: The time taken to perform the G1 MSM to generate point B1.
  • MSM B2: The time taken to perform the G2 MSM to generate point B2.
  • MSM C: The time taken to perform the G1 MSM to generate point C.
  • MSM H: The time taken to perform the G1 MSM to generate point H.
  • Proof valid?: Whether the proof is valid.
